Help & FAQ

Answers to the questions we hear most.

Getting started & methodology
What makes PrepHooks different from standard textbooks?
PrepHooks uses a question-first active recall model. Instead of passively reading hundreds of pages, you start by solving high-yield exam scenarios. Research shows that retrieving information during practice builds stronger neural pathways and dramatically increases retention for licensure and standardized exams.
What subjects and exams are available on PrepHooks?
PrepHooks offers comprehensive question banks, practice modules, and detailed lesson breakdowns across professional licensure exams (including Nursing & Midwifery) as well as university entry and standardized secondary syllabi. New topics and lesson modules are continuously added and synced directly to your library.
How do I track my progress?
Your diagnostic history is automatically recorded in your PrepMap analytics tab. You can view your overall accuracy, topic mastery percentages, completed lesson modules, and revision recommendations in real time.
Account & subscription management
How do I unlock full access to all topics and lessons?
Select any locked topic in your library or visit your profile settings to initiate a subscription plan. Once completed, your account state instantly unlocks full access to all corresponding practice modules, diagnostic tests, and rationales.
Where can I see my subscription status and expiration date?
Go to the You (Profile) tab in the main navigation bar. Your active access status and exact expiration date are clearly displayed alongside your profile handle.
Does my progress sync across multiple devices?
Yes. Your account state, test history, active subscription, and PrepMap progress are synced securely to our cloud database. Simply log in with your email and password on any supported mobile device to resume right where you left off.
Troubleshooting & payment issues
I completed a payment, but my content is still locked. What should I do?
  1. Return to the Home screen and pull down on the page to trigger a manual state refresh.
  2. If your account does not update within a few minutes, go to Profile → Report a Problem or contact support at support@prephooks.com with your account email and transaction payment reference. Our support team will manually verify and activate your access state.
I found a typo, incorrect answer, or broken explanation. How can I report it?
We strive for total content accuracy. If you notice a questionable question or rationale while practicing, tap the flag/report icon within the test interface or navigate to Profile → Report a Problem to submit feedback directly to our medical and academic content team.
