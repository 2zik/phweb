Help · 03

Using the App

PrepHooks tracks your performance on every question. It identifies your strengths and weaknesses at the topic level, then serves you questions that target your weakest areas. The more you use it, the more it understands where you need the most work.

Your Readiness Score is a single number that shows how prepared you are for your exam. It combines two factors:

  • Exposure: how much of the syllabus you have covered
  • Proficiency: how well you perform on what you have studied

Think of it like a speedometer — it tells you where you stand at a glance.

The Readiness Score is calculated per topic and then averaged across all topics:

  • Completeness: How much of the topic's question pool you have attempted
  • Accuracy: Your percentage of correct answers
  • Confidence: A reliability measure based on how many questions you have answered

Readiness = Completeness × (Accuracy × Confidence)

You can track your progress in two ways:

  • PrepMap: A visual syllabus map that shows which topics you have completed, which are in progress, and which are untouched
  • Progress History: Week-over-week trend of your Readiness Score (Premium feature)

The PrepMap is a visual, topic-by-topic representation of your syllabus. Each topic shows your proficiency as a percentage. You can see at a glance:

  • Which topics you have mastered (green)
  • Which topics need work (yellow or red)
  • Which topics you have not started (gray)

PrepHooks tells you exactly what to focus on:

  • Your PrepMap shows your weakest topics in red
  • Premium users get a Personalized Study Path that ranks topics by impact — showing you which ones will improve your score the most

After answering any question, tap View Explanation to see the full rationale. For Premium users, the AI explanation layer provides personalized insights based on your history.

Go to the CBT Simulation section from your dashboard. You can choose a subject, set the number of questions, and start a timed or untimed practice test. It mirrors the actual exam experience.
